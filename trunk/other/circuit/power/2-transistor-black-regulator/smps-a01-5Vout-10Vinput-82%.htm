The 2-transistor Black regulator

App a01; +5v output, good regulation


Specs

  • 82% efficiency (from 60mA to 200mA out)
  • Very good 5v regulation
  • Cheap circuit
  • Input: 10v to 18v
  • Output: 5v @ 350mA
